Turn a negative log into a positive goal-setter.

Reclamation Counter: When a user logs a task, instead of just saying "Wasted 45 minutes on Doomscrolling," have a feature that says: "With that 45 minutes, you could have finished 1 lesson of Spanish or walked 3 kilometers."

Budgets for Wasted Time: Let users set a "guilty pleasure" budget (e.g., max 2 hours of gaming/social media a week). The app warns them as they approach the limit.
